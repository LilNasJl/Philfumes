Welcome to PhilFumes

Founded on November of 2016, the PhilFumes Petroleum Corporation (PPC) envisioned to bridge sustainable, economical, and accessible source of energy among the households of Davao Region. Officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in May 2018, PPC has since made significant strides in realizing its corporate vision. As they put into work the corporate’s dream, they have established its first station in Maco, Davao de Oro last 2017. Delivering tons of gasoline in this area made its name known among locals which has expanded access across neighbouring cities.

Determined to broaden its regional reach, PPC strategically built one station after another, achieving a remarkable milestone of 10 stations within a span of six years. This expansion wasn't limited to one province; it extended PPC's presence to four provinces. What started with a pioneering team of three employees has now blossomed into a workforce of 100 personnel, all dedicated to serving with utmost integrity. Anchored in the company’s tagline, “Powers your way,” PPC fuel every motorist’s journey, ignite every personnel’s vision, and blaze up the company’s drive to deliver exceptional key products and customer service. As PPC continues to grow, it remains steadfast in its dedication to providing a reliable energy source that empowers both individuals and communities in the Davao Region and beyond.
